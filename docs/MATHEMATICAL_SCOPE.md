# Mathematical scope of the certificate

The machine-checkable supplement verifies:

- the three-dimensional source map and `det JF = -2`;
- three pairwise-distinct rational preimages of one value;
- the normalization `K=(F3/2,F2,F1)` and determinant one;
- 18 elementary BCW degree-lowering row identities;
- reconstruction of the dimension-39 map and its `I+A2+A3` decomposition;
- term counts `39/47/38` in degrees one, two and three;
- the dimension-79 homogeneous-cubic block construction;
- final block-Jacobian identities and the rational triple collision.

It does not formalize every geometric theorem in the manuscript, re-prove the general BCW/Yagzhev theorem, settle attribution or replace peer review.
