# Mathematical scope of the certificate

The machine-checkable supplement verifies:

- the three-dimensional source map;
- `det JF = -2` by exact differentiation;
- three pairwise-distinct rational preimages of one value;
- the normalization `K=(F3/2,F2,F1)` and determinant one;
- 18 elementary BCW degree-lowering row identities;
- reconstruction of the dimension-39 map;
- its decomposition as identity plus quadratic plus cubic parts;
- the term counts `39/47/38` in degrees one, two and three;
- the dimension-79 homogeneous-cubic block construction;
- the final block-Jacobian identities and rational triple collision.

It does not formalize every geometric theorem in the manuscript, re-prove
the general BCW/Yagzhev theorem from first principles, settle attribution or
replace peer review.
