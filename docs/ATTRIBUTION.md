# Attribution and chronology

This repository separates three categories:

1. The explicit counterexample and its public mathematical provenance.
2. Independent reproduction of the determinant and rational collision.
3. The algebraic and geometric analysis, exposition and reproducibility infrastructure included here.

Third-party discussions, repositories or pending pull requests do not imply institutional endorsement. Priority and attribution must follow primary records and subsequent scholarly review. The manuscript is a preprint and has not been peer reviewed.

Contributors should document imported identities, normalizations and formalizations and avoid presenting third-party work as original.
