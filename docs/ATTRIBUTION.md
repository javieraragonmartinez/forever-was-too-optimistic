# Attribution and chronology

This repository separates three categories:

1. The explicit counterexample and its public mathematical provenance.
2. Independent reproduction of the determinant and rational collision.
3. The algebraic and geometric analysis, exposition and reproducibility
   infrastructure included in this release.

The existence of third-party discussions, repositories or pending pull
requests does not imply institutional endorsement. Priority and attribution
must follow primary records and subsequent scholarly review. The manuscript
is a preprint and has not been peer reviewed.

Contributors should document any imported identity, normalization or
formalization and avoid presenting a third-party result as original work.
